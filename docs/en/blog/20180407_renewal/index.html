Website renewal

April 7, 2018

I’ve changed the generating engine of my website from Wordpress to Hugo with my original bibliography manager morepo. The nice things about using a static website generator are that you don’t need CGI on your server, and that you can have a simpler way of managing your contents. The multi-lingual support of Hugo also impresses me a lot.

I’m now still working on migrating the contents from the old website, which is taking more of my time.
